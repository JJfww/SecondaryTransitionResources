ChoiceMaker Self-Determination Transition Curriculum Modules & Materials

The  ChoiceMaker Self-Determination Transition Curriculum Matrix is a guide for using these lessons. Results from the ChoiceMaker Self-Determination Assessment can be mapped onto the Matrix to identify students' self-determination needs.

Choosing Goals video is a 10 minute video that may be used to introduce the topic. The video was made by individuals with IEPs. Hair/clothes shows that it is outdated, but the topics are still relevant. Maybe a class or two would want to create an updated video? Many transition skills could be integrated in the endeavor!
